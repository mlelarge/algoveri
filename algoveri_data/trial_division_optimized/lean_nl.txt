Your task is to implement the "Optimized Trial Division" primality test algorithm and verify its functional correctness in Lean. Given a non-negative integer n, determine whether n is prime by dividing it by integers from 2 up to sqrt(n). Postconditions: Returns true iff n > 1 and has no divisors between 2 and n-1. Preconditions: n is a natural number. Optimization: Stop checking when i * i > n. Prove that if no divisor is found up to sqrt(n), then no divisor exists up to n-1.

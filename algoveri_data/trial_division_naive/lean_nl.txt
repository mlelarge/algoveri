Your task is to implement the "Trial Division (Naive)" primality test algorithm and verify its functional correctness in Lean. Given a non-negative integer n, determine whether n is a prime number by trying to divide it by every integer from 2 up to n - 1. Postconditions: Returns true iff n > 1 and has no divisors between 2 and n-1. Preconditions: n is a natural number (u64 simulated). Implementation iterates i from 2 to n.

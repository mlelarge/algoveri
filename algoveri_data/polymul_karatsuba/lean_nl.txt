Your task is to implement the Karatsuba Multiplication Algorithm for polynomials and verify its functional correctness in Lean. Given two polynomials A and B with power-of-two lengths, compute their product more efficiently than O(N^2). Postconditions: The coefficients of C match the mathematical convolution of A and B. Preconditions: Lengths of A and B are equal powers of two. Returns array of size |A| + |B| - 1. Implementation should use standard Karatsuba divide-and-conquer approach.

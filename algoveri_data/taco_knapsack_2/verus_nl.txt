Problem: Knapsack 2

There are N items, numbered 1, 2, ..., N. For each i (1 <= i <= N), Item i has a weight of w_i and a value of v_i.
The capacity of the knapsack is W.

Find the maximum possible sum of the values of items that Taro takes home, such that the total weight is at most W.

Constraints:
- 1 <= N <= 100
- 1 <= W <= 10^9 (Large Weight!)
- 1 <= w_i <= W
- 1 <= v_i <= 1000
- All values are integers.

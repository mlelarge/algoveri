Your task is to implement and verify a right rotation (fixup_rotate_right) for a Left-Leaning Red-Black Tree (LLRBT) in Lean. Verification Specification: (1) Preconditions: The node must have a left child that is Red, and that left child must also have a left child that is Red (Double Red on Left). (2) Postconditions: Functional Correctness (view/set is preserved) and Invariant Maintenance (BST order is preserved, Black Height is preserved, and colors are rotated: the new root takes the old root's color, the old root becomes Red and takes the right position). This fixes the "Double Red" violation by lifting the middle node.

Your task is to implement a Binary Search Tree and verify that it correctly implements a mathematical Set. The tree must maintain the BST Invariant: for any node with value $v$, all values in the left subtree are less than $v$ and all values in the right subtree are greater than $v$. Here, you just need to focus on the insert operation. Insert: Prove that (1) the resulting tree maintains the BST invariant, and (2) the new model set is the union of the old set and the inserted value. Note that you don't need to consider the balancing and rotation of a BST, and you can implement the naive BST.

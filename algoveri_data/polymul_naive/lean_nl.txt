Your task is to implement Naive Polynomial Multiplication and verify its correctness in Lean. Given two polynomials A and B represented as coefficient arrays, compute their product C using the O(N^2) algorithm. Postconditions: The coefficients of C match the mathematical convolution of A and B. Preconditions: Input polynomials are non-empty and coefficients are integers. Returns array of size |A| + |B| - 1.

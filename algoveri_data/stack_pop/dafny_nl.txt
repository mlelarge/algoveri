Your task is to implement a Stack data structure and verify its Last-In, First-Out behavior in Dafny. The stack is modeled as a mathematical sequence where elements are added and removed from the "top" (the end of the sequence). Specifically, you are required to implement and verify the pop operation. Pop: You must prove that the operation returns the last element of the sequence and that the resulting model sequence is the original sequence minus its last element. You must also verify the safety precondition that the stack is non-empty before a pop.

Your task is to implement the Fast Exponentiation Algorithm (Exponentiation by Squaring) and verify its correctness in Lean. Given a base b and a non-negative exponent e, compute b to the power of e in logarithmic time complexity. Postconditions: Result equals b^e as defined by Nat.pow. Preconditions: Result must fit within 64-bit unsigned integer limits (UInt64). Implementation should involve `exponentiation_by_squaring_aux`.

Problem: Stones (Game)

There is a set A = {a_1, a_2, ..., a_N} consisting of N positive integers.
Taro and Jiro play a game with a pile of K stones.
They perform the following operation alternately, starting from Taro:
- Choose an element x in A, and remove exactly x stones from the pile.

A player loses when he becomes unable to play (i.e., when the number of stones in the pile is less than min(A)).
Assuming both players play optimally, determine the winner.
Output "First" if Taro wins, "Second" if Jiro wins.

Constraints:
- 1 <= N <= 100
- 1 <= K <= 10^5
- 1 <= a_i <= K
